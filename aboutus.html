About HexaTP

Our Story

HexaTP was founded by six professionals from finance, economics, legal, science, and technology domains who spent significant parts of their careers in transfer pricing and international taxation.

With over 100 years of combined experience across industries, our experts provide comprehensive knowledge covering legislation, implementation, and litigation support.

Why We Built HexaTP

Businesses depend on reliable data to make critical financial decisions. Our platform combines domain expertise with advanced data analytics to deliver accurate benchmarking and compliance solutions.

Our Mission

To provide a Transfer Pricing Knowledge Intelligence platform that supports professionals, corporates, and regulatory authorities with accurate and reliable information.

Our Vision

To empower businesses and professionals with well-researched knowledge that helps them create long-term value and informed business decisions.
